Key Regulatory Requirements & Approval Processes

Services & Application Processes

These are the key regulatory requirements and approval processes that schools must complete and obtain from the relevant authority before commencing operations or implementing major institutional changes.

Government Permit to Operate

Required for all private schools and learning centers before they may legally operate.

Learn More ›

Recognition of Schools and Academic Programs

Ensures that schools and academic programs meet the standards set by DepEd.

Learn More ›

Tuition and Other School Fees Increase (TOSFI) Applications

Apply for authority to increase tuition and other school fees in compliance with DepEd guidelines.

Learn More ›

Establishment of New Schools, Branch Campuses, and Additional Academic Programs

Secure approval to open new schools, branch campuses, or offer new academic programs.

Learn More ›

Change of School Name, Ownership, or Location

Request approval for changes in school name, ownership, or transfer of school location.

Learn More ›

Senior High School Permit Applications

Obtain a permit to offer Senior High School programs in compliance with DepEd policies.

Learn More ›

School Calendar

Submit the school year calendar for review, including class opening, holidays, examinations, breaks, school activities, and school day counts.
